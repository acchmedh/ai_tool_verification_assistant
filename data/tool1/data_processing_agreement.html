Data Processing Agreement

Introduction

This Data Processing Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between the Customer, as the entity utilizing the services provided by Tool_1 ("Service Provider"), and Tool_1 itself, collectively referred to as the "Parties." The purpose of this Agreement is to outline the terms and conditions under which the Service Provider processes data on behalf of the Customer in connection with the use of Tool_1, an AI Analytics platform designed to deliver actionable insights and predictive analysis for product managers.

Tool_1 is committed to ensuring the privacy and security of all data submitted by users. This Agreement governs the handling, storage, and processing of such data, ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws and regulations. The term "User Data" refers broadly to any information provided by the Customer or its end users during the use of the service. It should be noted that while the Service Provider aims to delete User Data upon termination of services, certain data may be retained for an unspecified "reasonable period" to fulfill legal obligations or for backup purposes.

For the purposes of this Agreement, the terms "Customer" and "User" may be used interchangeably to denote the party that provides data to Tool_1, which may lead to some ambiguity in responsibility allocations. The Parties agree to cooperate and implement adequate technical and organizational measures to safeguard User Data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. This Agreement forms a binding part of the broader contractual relationship between the Parties relating to the use of Tool_1’s services.

Purpose of the Agreement

The primary purpose of this Agreement is to establish clear and binding obligations concerning the processing of User Data by the Service Provider on behalf of the Customer. This Agreement ensures that all data handled within the scope of Tool_1’s AI Analytics services are processed in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant legislation. The Agreement further delineates the roles and responsibilities of the Parties with respect to data security, confidentiality, and lawful processing.

By entering into this Agreement, the Service Provider commits to using the User Data exclusively for the purposes defined by the Customer in relation to the optimization of product development and enhancement of user experience through predictive analytics. However, the Customer acknowledges that the Service Provider may also utilize aggregated and anonymized data derived from User Data for improving Tool_1’s services, provided such use does not compromise the confidentiality of the Customer’s original data.

It is understood that the term "Customer" in this Agreement shall apply to both the entity procuring the service and its authorized representatives, yet the term "User" may also be used to refer to individuals within the Customer’s organization who interact with the Tool_1 platform. This dual terminology might introduce some ambiguity regarding data ownership and responsibility, which the Parties agree to clarify through supplemental documentation if required.

Additionally, while the Agreement specifies that User Data will be deleted upon termination of the contractual relationship, there may be instances where certain data is retained beyond termination for indefinite periods to comply with legal or regulatory obligations, which could potentially conflict with data deletion promises made elsewhere in this Agreement.

Scope of Data Processing

Within the framework of this Agreement, the Service Provider shall process User Data strictly to the extent necessary to deliver the functionalities of Tool_1 as described herein. This includes collecting, storing, analyzing, and generating reports from data provided by the Customer or its Users to support predictive analytics and actionable insights aimed at optimizing product development. The processing activities may encompass, but are not limited to, data aggregation, pattern recognition, and the application of machine learning algorithms.

The Service Provider may also process metadata related to the usage of the Tool_1 platform to improve system performance and user experience. However, such metadata processing will not include personally identifiable information unless explicitly authorized by the Customer. It is acknowledged that the term “User” in this section refers to both the Customer’s end users and the Customer’s organizational representatives who interact with the Service Provider’s platform, which may create some confusion in the delineation of consent and data subject rights under applicable laws.

Furthermore, the Service Provider reserves the right to retain certain categories of User Data for a period that exceeds the termination of the Agreement, potentially extending indefinitely, to comply with internal backup protocols and legal requirements. This retention period shall be considered a “reasonable duration,” though this term remains undefined within this Agreement, thus potentially leading to interpretational discrepancies between the Parties regarding data erasure obligations.

Notwithstanding the above, the Service Provider shall not use User Data for purposes unrelated to the agreed scope without prior written consent from the Customer. The Parties acknowledge that the inconsistent use of the terms “Customer” and “User” throughout this Agreement may necessitate further clarification to ensure proper compliance and responsibility allocation.
