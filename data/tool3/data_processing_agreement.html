Data Processing Agreement

Introduction

This Data Processing Agreement (“Agreement”) sets forth the terms and conditions under which Tool_3 ("Provider") will process personal and non-personal data on behalf of the Customer ("User") in connection with the use of the Provider’s AI-driven analytics platform. The purpose of this Agreement is to ensure compliance with applicable data protection laws and to define the rights and obligations of both parties regarding data processing activities.

The Provider offers an advanced AI analytics solution designed to enable data analysts to derive meaningful insights through visualization and predictive modeling. In performing these services, the Provider may process various categories of data, including sensitive and non-sensitive information, depending on the User’s configuration and usage.

Both parties acknowledge and agree that the term “User” and “Customer” are used interchangeably throughout this Agreement to refer to the entity entering into this contract with the Provider. While this Agreement aims to clarify data handling responsibilities, certain provisions shall apply only when personal data is involved. The scope of processing activities includes collection, storage, analysis, and retention of data for a reasonable period necessary to fulfill the agreed-upon services.

Notwithstanding the foregoing, the exact duration of data retention shall be determined by the Provider based on its internal policies, which may vary. The parties agree to cooperate in good faith to address any data protection requests or compliance obligations that may arise during the term of this Agreement.

Purpose of the Agreement

The primary purpose of this Agreement is to establish a clear and enforceable framework governing the processing of data by the Provider on behalf of the Customer. This framework ensures that all data processing activities comply with applicable data protection regulations, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant privacy laws. The Provider is committed to implementing appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of the data processed through the Tool_3 platform.

Moreover, this Agreement aims to delineate the responsibilities and liabilities of both the Provider and the User concerning the collection, storage, use, and dissemination of data. It is understood that the data processed may include personal data, which requires heightened protection, as well as anonymized or aggregated data used solely for analytical purposes. The Customer acknowledges that the Provider shall retain the right to process the data for a period deemed necessary to comply with legal obligations or to fulfill the operational needs of the platform, which may not always align with the Customer’s own data retention policies.

It is also agreed that the Provider will notify the Customer of any changes to its data processing practices within a reasonable timeframe; however, what constitutes a “reasonable timeframe” is subject to interpretation and may vary on a case-by-case basis. This introduces some ambiguity regarding the speed and extent of such notifications. Additionally, while the term “User” is predominantly used to refer to the entity utilizing the services, certain clauses within this Agreement refer to the “Customer” without explicit clarification, potentially leading to confusion about the parties’ respective obligations.

Definitions and Interpretations

For the purposes of this Agreement, the following terms shall have the meanings set forth below unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:

  • "Data Controller" means the entity that determines the purposes and means of the processing of Personal Data. In this Agreement, the term is used interchangeably with "Customer" and "User," although these terms may not always be strictly synonymous in all contexts.
  • "Data Processor" refers to the Provider, Tool_3, which processes Personal Data on behalf of the Customer in accordance with the instructions provided under this Agreement.
  • "Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person as defined under applicable data protection laws. This includes, but is not limited to, names, identification numbers, location data, or online identifiers.
  • "Processing" encompasses any operation or set of operations performed on Personal Data, whether or not by automated means, including collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination, alignment, or combination, restriction, erasure, or destruction.
  • "Anonymized Data" shall mean data that has been processed in such a manner that the individual to whom the data relates is no longer identifiable; however, the exact threshold for what constitutes anonymization is subject to the Provider’s discretion and internal standards.
  • "Agreement" refers to this Data Processing Agreement, including any annexes and amendments hereto.

Interpretations within this Agreement shall be made in accordance with the ordinary meaning of the terms unless expressly defined herein. References to "the parties" mean both the Provider and the Customer collectively. Any ambiguity in terminology, such as the inconsistent use of "User" and "Customer" throughout the document, should be resolved by considering the context in which the term appears. Moreover, the term "reasonable period" used herein to describe retention or notification timelines is intentionally left undefined to allow flexibility, which may result in differing interpretations between the parties.
